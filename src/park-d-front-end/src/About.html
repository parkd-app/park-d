How to use Park'd

Enable location access if you would like to use the built in navigation feature.
If you enable location, the map will centre at your current location.

Click or tap the blue circle located on the parking lot you wish to use.
The spots in that parking lot will load, along with their status.
Green outlines are regular spots.
Blue outlines are accessible spots.
Red outlines are reserved spots.

Available spots will be coloured in green.
Occupied spots will be coloured in red.

If you've enabled location: Click or tap the spot you would like to use.
A live path will be displayed between you and your destination.
If you select an accessible or reserved space, you will need to confirm that you are authorized to use it.
